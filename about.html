About Adventist Health India

Learn about our mission, vision, values, and the dedicated team that makes our healthcare network possible.

Our Mission

Healing with Purpose

To provide comprehensive, compassionate healthcare services that promote physical, mental, and spiritual well-being, following the healing ministry of Jesus Christ. We are committed to serving all people regardless of their background, beliefs, or economic status, ensuring that quality healthcare is accessible to everyone in our communities.

Our Vision

A Future of Excellence

To be the leading healthcare network in India, recognized for excellence in medical care, innovation, and compassionate service to all communities. We envision a future where every individual has access to quality healthcare, where medical professionals work in harmony with faith-based principles, and where healing extends beyond physical treatment to encompass the whole person.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision and interaction in our healthcare delivery.

Compassion

We treat every patient with empathy, understanding, and genuine care, recognizing that healing involves both medical expertise and human connection.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in medical care, continuously improving our services and maintaining excellence in all aspects of healthcare delivery.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards, ensuring transparency, honesty, and accountability in all our interactions and decisions.

Respect

We honor the dignity of every individual, respecting diverse backgrounds, beliefs, and perspectives while providing culturally sensitive care.

Service

We are dedicated to serving our communities selflessly, putting the needs of patients and their families at the center of everything we do.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and methodologies to enhance patient care while maintaining our commitment to compassionate, personalized treatment.

Ownership & Management

Organizational Structure

Registration: The Medical Trust of the Seventh-day Adventists is registered under Registration No. 515 of 1999, establishing our legal foundation and commitment to healthcare service.

Governing Body: Our organization operates under the guidance of the General Conference of Seventh-day Adventists, Southern Asia Division, ensuring alignment with global healthcare standards and Adventist health principles.

Management Philosophy: We believe in decentralized management that empowers local hospital leadership while maintaining consistent standards and shared values across our network. This approach allows each hospital to serve its unique community needs while benefiting from the collective expertise and resources of our entire network.
